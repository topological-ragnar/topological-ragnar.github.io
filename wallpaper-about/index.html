Wallpapers and Crystal Symmetries

2018-2019

A crystal is a spontaneously self-assembled repeating pattern of atoms. What does it mean that a pattern is repeating? It means that it has a unit cell, which is a small region which generates the whose crystal by a discrete group of translations. Thus a crystal is a pattern with a translation symmetry.

Crystals can have other symmetries as well, such as rotations and reflections. In two dimensions there are exactly 17 ways to combine translations with these other symmetries. In three dimensions there are 230. In four dimensions there are still more, and there are always finitely many.
